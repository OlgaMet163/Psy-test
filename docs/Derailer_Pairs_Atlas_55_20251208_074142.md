# Derailer Pairs Atlas (55 combinations)

Development-oriented descriptions of how *two high stress traits* can combine across life domains. This is not a diagnostic instrument.

**Domains covered per pair:** Career, Business, Friendships, Hobbies, Romantic, Overall lifestyle, Health, Sports.

---

## 1) **VR+WA** — Volatile Reactivity + Worry-Driven Avoidance

**Signature:** alarm volatility

**Core loop:** uncertainty triggers worst-case thinking and hesitation → tone sharpens, patience drops, and reactions come fast → timing is missed and anxiety becomes the operating system under stress.

- **Career:** tone spikes, defensiveness to feedback, performance variance across weeks; analysis paralysis; overchecking; delays hard calls and conflict. Net effect: slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.
- **Business:** rapid pivots and escalation culture; urgency communicated with heat; slow launches; over-validation; missed windows from hesitation. Net effect: slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.
- **Friendships:** intensity cycles; sensitivity to slights; rupture–repair patterns; avoids direct talks; replays interactions; seeks certainty. Net effect: slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.
- **Hobbies:** frustration spikes; quits/pauses abruptly or pushes angrily; over-researches; delays starting; fear of doing it wrong. Net effect: slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.
- **Romantic:** arguments ignite quickly; push–pull closeness; fast escalation; rumination and reassurance loops; ambiguous cues feel threatening. Net effect: slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.
- **Overall lifestyle:** bursts and crashes; routines swing with mood and stress; narrower comfort routines; reduced novelty; mental load stays high. Net effect: slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.
- **Health:** sleep disruption/tension; coping volatility (comfort behaviors or overcontrol); insomnia/tension; difficulty relaxing; stress symptoms persist. Net effect: slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.
- **Sports:** overreaches after setbacks; heat with coaches/partners; volatile load choices; conservative progression; inconsistent blocks from fear (injury/failure). Net effect: slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.

**Strength edge (when regulated): risk awareness, urgency and energy.**  
**Derailer edge (when sustained): slow closure / rumination, high conflict temperature.**

## 2) **VR+IW** — Volatile Reactivity + Interpersonal Withdrawal

- **Health:** sleep disruption/tension; coping volatility (comfort behaviors or overcontrol); delays seeking help; under-communicates symptoms; low support use. Net effect: low transparency / disconnection, high conflict temperature.
- **Sports:** overreaches after setbacks; heat with coaches/partners; volatile load choices; trains alone; avoids coaching feedback; plateau risk from low input. Net effect: low transparency / disconnection, high conflict temperature.

**Derailer edge (when sustained): low transparency / disconnection, high conflict temperature.**
